Chapter 01: Senior High

The Beginning

Our story began in Grade 11 when we, a group of  students, became classmates without realizing that we were about to build a friendship that would last beyond the classroom. What started as simple conversations, sharing notes, helping each other with schoolwork, and laughing over the smallest things slowly turned into countless unforgettable memories. As we moved on to Grade 12 together, our bond grew even stronger through every challenge, celebration, and inside joke we shared. We became each other's support system, creating moments that made school feel like a second home. That's how Peeps was born—not just as the name of our friend group, but as a reminder that the best friendships often begin with ordinary classmates who choose to stay together through every chapter of life.

"It started with a shared pen and ended with a lifelong bond."
